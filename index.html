Change the routine, keep the cue and reward

Start Small

2-minute rule. Scale down until it takes 2 minutes. "Read 1 page" not "Read 30 minutes."
Never miss twice. One skip is accident. Two is new habit forming.
Identity-based habits. "I'm a reader" not "I want to read more."

Make It Obvious

Implementation intention. "I will [BEHAVIOR] at [TIME] in [LOCATION]."
Habit stacking. "After I [CURRENT HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT]."
Environment design. Put cues in sight. Want to read? Book on pillow.

Make It Attractive

Temptation bundling. Pair habit with something you enjoy. Podcast only while exercising.
Join a tribe. Surround yourself with people who have the habit.
Reframe mindset. "I get to" not "I have to."

Make It Easy

Reduce friction. Want to gym? Sleep in workout clothes.
Prime your environment. Set up the night before.
Automate when possible. Auto-save, auto-bill, meal prep.

Make It Satisfying

Track your habit. Visual streak is motivating. Don't break the chain.
Immediate reward. Delayed rewards don't reinforce well.
Celebrate small wins. Internal "yes!" after completing.
